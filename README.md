# BVD Outbreak Watch — Daily Situational Picture

Open-source daily situational picture of the 2026 Bundibugyo Ebola virus outbreak in the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Uganda.

**🔗 Live dashboard:** https://humsecwatch.github.io/bvd-2026-tracker/

## What this is
An aggregated, plain-language daily snapshot of the outbreak — case counts, country-by-country travel restrictions, news, and contextual background — pulling from publicly-available sources including the WHO, US CDC, ECDC, Africa CDC, and reputable news outlets.

Designed for humanitarian workers, researchers, and the interested public who need a one-page reference that links out to authoritative primary sources.

## What this isn't
Not officially affiliated with any UN agency, government, or humanitarian organisation. Provided for situational awareness only — not a substitute for official advisories from your organisation, employer, or relevant authorities. Verify any operational decision against the primary sources linked inline.
